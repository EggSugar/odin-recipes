Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

This kimchi fried rice recipe is delicious.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup well-fermented kimchi
  • 1 green onion, sliced, white and green separated
  • 1 tablesppon canola Oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame Oil
  • 1/4 cup ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang
  • 1 teaspoon butter

Steps

  1. Add canola oil to skillet over high heat.Add ground beef and white part of green onion and cook for 2 minutes.
  2. Add kimchi and gochujang into meat mixture and cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Add rice and cook for 4 minutes.
  4. Drizzle sesame oil over rice and stir.
  5. Melt butter in different skillet over medium-high heat. Crack egg in pan and cook until white is set and yolk is thick.
  6. Place egg over the fried rice.
  7. Enjoy
