Shrimp Scampi with Pasta

Description

Ingredients

  • 1(16 ounce) package linguine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 shallots, finely diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes(Optional)
  • 1 pound peeled shrimp
  • 1 pinch kosher salt and ground pepper
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley leaves
  • 1 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil, or to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients.
  2. Bring salted water to boil in large pot. Cook pasta until close to tender. Drain.
  3. Melt 2 tablespoons butter with 2 tablespoons olive oil in large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Cook shallots, garlic, and red pepper flakes in the butter and oil until shallows are translucent.
  5. Season shrimp with kosher salt and black pepper. Add the skillet and cook until pink. Remove shrimp and keep warm.
  6. Pour white wine and lemon juice into skillet and bring to a boil while scraping the browned bits of food off the skillet.
  7. Melt 2 table sppons butter in skillet, stir 2 tablespoons olive oil into butter and bring to simmer.
  8. Add linguine, shrimp, and parsley into the mixture and toss until coated. Season with salt and black pepper. Drizzle 1 teaspoon olive oil.
  9. Finished. Enjoy.
