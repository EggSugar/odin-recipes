Pizza Dough

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1(.25)package active dry yeast
  • 1 teasppon white sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Prepare all ingredients. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Pour warm water in a bowl. Add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until creamy.
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to mixture. Beat until smooth. Can use hand or use a stand mixer with dough hook.
  4. Let it rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Turn dough out onto lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.
  6. Spread crust with sauce and desired toppings
  7. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown. About 15 to 20 minutes.
  8. Remove from oven and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  9. Enjoy
